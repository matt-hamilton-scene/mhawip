Welcome to my website!

I'm Matt, a design focused interdisciplinary engineer currently working in the community energy and energy access sectors. I'm passionate about technology that will enable the transition to a sustainable, equitable, low-carbon economy; and I strive to bring clarity to the projects I work on. Outside of design engineering I enjoy type 2 fun, art, and football.

I graduated from Imperial College London's MEng Design Engineering program in 2020. I have experience working in the off-grid solar sector, and investigated Circular Economy Solutions for Off-Grid Solar for my master's thesis. In my final year of university I also co-founded Impact Games, a start-up that makes impact driven table top games

I'm delighted to currently be working as a Design Engineer at Scene Connect ltd. Scene are an Edinburgh based social enterprise which supports local energy futures through research, consultancy, and product development.
